About PetDoc

PetDoc is your pet’s health companion. We’re here to help you understand your pet’s health through AI-powered insights. Our goal is to make it easier for you to take the right steps in caring for your pet, even before or without visiting the vet. By simply entering symptoms, our tool offers possible diagnoses and action steps tailored to your pet's health.

Our Mission

We aim to transform pet care by providing an innovative and accessible way for pet owners to monitor their pets’ health. PetDoc is designed to give pet owners a fast, easy, and accurate way to assess possible health concerns of their pets. With our AI-driven platform, we make veterinary-level insights accessible with the touch of a button.

Our Vision

We picture a world where pet owners are empowered with the tools they need to take proactive care of their pets' health. PetDoc strives to be the ultimate solution for understanding pet health at home, reducing unnecessary vet visits, and as well improving pet well-being worldwide.

How We Help

Our symptom checker uses artificial intelligence to provide you with immediate feedback on your pet’s health based on the symptoms and additional information you enter. Whether it’s a minor concern or major concern, PetDoc helps you assess your pet’s condition and suggests whether a vet visit is needed.
